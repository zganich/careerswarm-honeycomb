### The Pitch (1-Pager)

**Problem:** Job seekers spend 4+ hours per application. Your staff is overwhelmed helping them write resumes and cover letters. Application volume is down, time-to-placement is up.

**Solution:** CareerSwarm ‚Äî AI career infrastructure that:
- Creates a Master Profile from their resume (once)
- Generates tailored resumes + cover letters for each job (minutes)
- Tracks all applications in one place
- Works 24/7 (supplements your staff, doesn't replace them)

**Pilot Proposal:**
- 50-100 job seekers for 90 days
- You provide: referrals, feedback
- We provide: full access, onboarding, support
- We measure: applications submitted, time-to-placement, satisfaction
- Investment: $15,000 (or allocate from existing discretionary/innovation funds)

**Why Now:** Job seekers expect AI-powered tools. First workforce board to offer this = competitive advantage.

## üèÜ #4: State RESEA Contract (Bigger, Slower)

### What is RESEA?
- **Reemployment Services and Eligibility Assessment**
- Federal program, administered by states
- Serves Unemployment Insurance (UI) claimants
- Requires them to engage with reemployment services
- States contract with providers to deliver these services

### Why It's Valuable
- **Steady volume** ‚Äî Thousands of UI claimants
- **Recurring contract** ‚Äî Multi-year agreements
- **State pays** ‚Äî Not dependent on individual sales

### Utah RESEA
- Administered by **Utah Department of Workforce Services (DWS)**
- Utah runs RESEA through their employment centers statewide
- Current providers: DWS centers + some contracted providers
- Contact: DWS Unemployment Insurance division or Workforce Development
- Website: jobs.utah.gov

**Utah note:** DWS is centralized (unlike some states with fragmented boards). This can be good ‚Äî one relationship at state level can unlock statewide access.

### What You'd Need to Build
- [ ] Reporting dashboard (outcomes by claimant)
- [ ] Integration with state systems (may require API)
- [ ] Compliance documentation
- [ ] Staff for support/account management

### Action Plan (Months 1-6)
- [ ] Research current RESEA providers in Colorado
- [ ] Contact CDLE to understand procurement process
- [ ] Start as subcontractor to existing provider (faster)
- [ ] Build toward direct contract in Year 2
